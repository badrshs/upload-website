Services

Do you have a mid-century home in need of a serious renovation? Maybe a newer home that needs a refresh? Metropolis Drafting and Construction can help you with any remodeling needs for your entire home. We are fully licensed and experienced in all aspects of home remodeling from kitchen and bath to room additions. From your project manager, to the onsite supervisor and to our fantastic crew, our team is experienced, competent and friendly.

All projects begin with a free in-home design consultation. One of our designers will visit your home to take measurements of your existing space and talk to you about what you hope to achieve. Your designer will work with you to clarify your vision and then return to our showroom to incorporate it into a design for you. A second meeting will allow you to view your vision in high definition 3-D and 2D drafts for your upcoming remodeling project. During the second visit you will also have the opportunity to view full kitchen, bath and shower galleries, as well as, select from hundreds of cabinetry styles, and countertop options, tile selections and all other materials needed for your project.

Once you have selected Metropolis Drafting and Construction for your remodeling project, you will be assigned a project manager. This person will work with you from start to finish to make certain your project goes off as smoothly as possible. Your project manager is your single point of contact for all aspects of your home remodel. They will help direct you during the selection of tile, grout, paint colors and fixtures. They will order the materials you need in the quantities required to complete your job on time and efficiently. They will keep you posted of any issues that arise during construction and answer any questions or concerns you have during the process.

Your project will also be assigned a field supervisor. This supervisor will work with the project manager to coordinate the scheduling of the various trades needed to complete your job. He will ensure that all work is in compliance with state and local municipality building codes and in accordance with the specifications of your design.

So when you want to refresh the look of your home, update the flooring to wood or laminate wood flooring, install new molding and paint the interior, open some walls to reconfigure the home layout or something as simple as upgrading all your windows to dual pane vinyl windows, Metropolis Drafting and Construction is the right address for all your remodeling needs.

Patio Covers

Full Shade Solid Patio Covers are the most popular in the southern California area. A full shade patio
cover provides shade for your outdoor living space so that you may still enjoy the refreshing breeze. It
serves to eliminate direct sunlight and allows you to take full advantage of your outdoor area in all
-weather types. Plus the look of a solid patio cover adds a stylish look to your home’s exterior.

Shading Options

The tremendous flexibility of our patio covers lets you select the amount of shade your solid patio cover
or partial lattice shade patio cover provides, from 60% to 40% to shade. You can even combine them to give
your patio area an ideal amount of shade for your personal comfort.


Metropolis drafting and construction offers our customers Superiority and Simplicity when it comes to patio
covers. The difference is clear when – unlike wood – our Full Shade Patio Cover is never in need of paint
or repair.

Solid Style

Here in southern California with our intense sun, Booth Built Solid Patio Products offer complete shade and total protection of your patio. The insulated laminate roof panels (LRP) are made with expanded polystyrene (EPS) core sandwiched between embossed aluminum panels. The flat pan cover is designed with heavy gauge aluminum and embossed to match perfectly with the lattice work.

Partial Shade Patio Covers

Lattice patio covers are becoming the most popular shade product in Southern California. A partial shade lattice patio covers are manufactured from strong, durable, lightweight aluminum embossed with a cedar texture to capture the look of perfectly cut wood. Although, it does not protect you from the outdoor elements, it serves to reduced direct sunlight. Plus the look of a partial shade classic lattice patio adds a stylish look to your home’s exterior.

Degree of Shade

By simply adjusting lattice tube spacing, a shade factor of 60-40 percent can be achieved. Let’s for a moment assume 2″ x 2″ lattice tubes. Rafters and beams are taken into consideration when calculating the shade coefficients. With our lattice patio covers you can regulate the amount of shade and sun you want while enjoying the outdoors.
