QAQC (Quality Assurance and Quality Control Guideline)

Background:

JIM Properties Ltd./JIM Business Associates has served more than four years in Bangladesh with major multinational and national companies. From the very beginning, JIM Properties Ltd./JIM Business Associates has been always served with quality as per customer requirement. The Company is now maintaining projects from two different project offices in Dhaka division along with Head office. So, it became mandatory to develop a guideline to ensure the quality of service and control accordingly from and on a same platform. The QAQC guidelines describe the process to control the quality of sites in terms of design, implementation, maintenance and documentation.

Establishing the QA/QC Criterion:

In order to define the criteria by which the various factors will be monitored for performance, an understanding of the project’s objective must be explored. According to services offered by JIM Properties Ltd./JIM Business Associates most projects have following broad sections.

1. Planning & Design
2. Procurement
3. Proper Material Storage/Management
4. Technical (Implementation & Maintenance)
5. Documentation
All the mentioned sections/departments possess their own monitoring mechanism to ensure.
the delivery according to customer or management requirements. Still, as of broader capacity we are intend to have central monitoring system by QA/QC department.
