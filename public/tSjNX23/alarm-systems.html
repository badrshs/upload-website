About monitored security alarms

Most home security alarms are designed for two purposes:

  • To alert you in the event of a break-in or household emergency

With ADT monitoring, alarms help to do all that and more. The high-decibel siren included with every monitoring package gives you the upper hand when you need to react quickly. The moment your doorway or motion sensors notice irregular movement, you’ll be alerted. The faster you can react in an emergency, the better off you’ll be.
