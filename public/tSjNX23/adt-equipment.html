ADT Monitored Equipment

With monitored alarm systems from ADT, security innovation comes standard.

ADT monitoring helps you protect the people and things you care about most by using the latest technology and cutting-edge security equipment. Alarm systems monitored by ADT offer 24-hour electronic monitoring that can detect break-ins, fire, carbon monoxide and other security threats.

Every ADT monitored security system comes with standard equipment that includes

Digital keypad

Maintain complete control with the wireless keypad, the nerve center of your home security system. The easy-to-use design lets you arm and disarm your system, adjust settings and more. The keypad can also be programmed for one-touch access to police, fire and medical dispatch.

Entryway contacts

Monitor your home's doors and windows with the Three Points of Protection. If the security system isn't armed, the control panel makes a sound when someone opens a door or window. However, if the system is armed and someone tries to force their way into your home, the entryway sensors send a signal to your keypad, which instantly activates the high-decibel alarm.

Interior motion detecto

Get extra protection for a foyer, hallway or room filled with valuables with an ADT monitored motion detector. The device’s infrared technology detects when someone enters a protected area of your home. The motion detector also features pet-friendly design to reduce the risk of your animal companions triggering false alarms.

Backup battery

Thanks to the backup battery, losing power doesn’t have to mean losing home security monitoring. The device automatically switches on in the event of a power outage and gives you up to 12 hours of monitoring coverage.

Wireless keychain remote

Enjoy convenient, versatile control over your home security system with the wireless keychain remote. The keychain remote features arm, disarm and panic buttons that make it easy to operate your security system up to 75 feet away from the digital keypad.

ADT yard sign and window decals

Customers aren't the only ones who know that ADT offers top-of-the-line monitoring service. Criminals know it too, which is why displaying your ADT yard sign and window decals could help prevent you from becoming a target.

In addition, all monitoring packages come with professional installation and the ADT Quality Service Plan covering all newly installed equipment.
