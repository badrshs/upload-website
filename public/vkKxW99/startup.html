We could spend hours extolling the virtues of the Millennial Lawyer App, but we would rather show you.

The Millennial Lawyer app redifines the Legal Tech landscape by providing instantaneous legal information to citizens in relation to all areas of their lives.

Users can pose questions and receive answers in real time. With the use of automatic speech recognition technology, the App is able to transcribe human speech into text and access a database of plain language resources to respond to inquiries.

The Millennial Lawyer identifies language that is linked to a specific area of the law so that you do not have to. It is like having a lawyer in your pocket!

Access to Justice

The development of Millennial Lawyer was done as a response to the access to justice crisis that plagues legal communities all over the globe. We believe that by informing people on the legal process we can help them better navigate the world they live in.

Millennial Lawyer was conceived by Amanda Wurah. As a law student, she gained an awareness of the importance of access to justice as a fundamental component of the principle of rule of law in Australia and beyond. She had developped the Millennial Lawyer prototype shortly after her graduation from the University of McGill Faculty of Law. In October 2018, the app was launched in Melbourne, Australia.

Our goal is to inform users from all demographics on their legal rights. The development of Millennial Lawyer started out of a conviction that the justice system should be less complex and more accessible. We provide a platform that is easy to navigate and understand. We are constantly striving to create a faster and more seamless user experience.
