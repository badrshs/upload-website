BOOR is an indie and casual strategy game. It is developed under the banner of DazlogStudio for Microsoft Windows. It is going to be released on 14th February, 2017 and Badland Games will release this game.

This game is all about a human colony which is known as Eden and it is far from Earth. You will see that the settlers of the colony made an artificial intelligence BOOR to help them. This machine instead turns against them and kills everybody.

When a little girl will land on this planet of Eden accidentally she will find that everybody there is dead except a few survivors and BOOR has occupied the planet. Now this girl has landed there and she must fight this deadly AI so that she can survive. She has the ability to multiply herself and this is the only way she can beat this Artificial Intelligence and reclaim the world.

Its an enjoyable 2D puzzle-plat-former game and there is a whole beautiful and strange world for you to discover. This game consists of many challenging levels which really test your abilities. You will also unravel the mystery of Eden and enjoy the detailed hand drawn levels which are visually rich.
