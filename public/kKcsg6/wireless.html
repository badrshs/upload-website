ADT CellGuard® – Stay Connected with Wireless Monitoring

The value of a home security system comes down to more than just dependability; it’s also about versatility. You not only need a system you can depend on, but one that can help keep your home safe no matter the situation. That’s where ADT Safewatch® CellGuard® really outshines the competition!

ADT CellGuard® maintains your vital link with an ADT alarm monitoring center through the use of radio signals, much like your cellphone. What this means is that you won’t need to worry about owning or purchasing a landline in order to enjoy the convenience and dependability of ADT’s 24/7 monitoring. ADT CellGuard® keeps your home system connected to ADT monitoring even in the absence of a landline – or in the event of life’s little surprises.

After all, landlines can be disrupted by thunderstorms or even damaged in construction but, thanks to ADT CellGuard®, you don’t have to worry about staying connected. You also won’t have to go through the hassle of having a landline for the sake of staying connected, not with ADT Safewatch® CellGuard®.

As it stands, many Americans don’t have landlines and rely solely on cellular and mobile devices for communication. ADT cellular monitoring with ADT CellGuard® allows homeowners to enjoy the same safety and security as a landline monitoring connection without having to spend extra money on a landline they either don’t need or want.

If you’re a homeowner looking to better safeguard your home, consider a monitoring package with ADT CellGuard®. Cost may vary depending on which package you choose.

CellGuard®: ADT Cellular Alarm Monitoring

Homeowners looking to switch to ADT can enjoy the peace of mind and convenience of ADT cellular monitoring. ADT offers a variety of packages, three of which offer ADT CellGuard®. CellGuard® is also available as an optional backup to a landline connection with the other two packages.

The landline is the vital component necessary for many security systems to stay in touch with monitoring services. However, ADT cellular monitoring brings some versatility to the company’s legacy of dependability. For landline phone users, ADT CellGuard® is more than just a backup. It’s a versatile feature designed to weather a variety of situations that might knock out a landline connection – including the weather.

As a landline backup, ADT cellular monitoring helps prevent lapses in alarm monitoring coverage that can be caused by any number of circumstances, including but not limited to:

  • Construction mishaps
  • Storms
  • Damaged or fallen lines
  • Accidents

ADT CellGuard® helps maintain that vital link to the monitoring centers if your landline and lifeline quits.

You can find out more about ADT CellGuard® prices by calling today for more information. There’s no special contract necessary and no need to set up a cellular service with a third party.
