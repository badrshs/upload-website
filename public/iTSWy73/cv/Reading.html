Reading

I love books! We read to know we're not alone.The more that you read, the more things you will know. The more that you learn, the more places you'll go.
I used to say that I find television very educating.Every time somebody turns on the set, I go into the other room and read a book!
A book is not just a collection of words, it is a perception portrayed on blank sheets. You know, getting you the feelings of blowing winds and rains and sun and flowers around, the smiles, the tears, the notional links with the characters and make them all alive, while you read.
Reading a book is like going on a great journey. You don't know what'll happen, but something is bound to change. And for me, that change has always been good.
I have read more than 100 books, each book tought me something.
