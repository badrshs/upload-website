It is important to maintain the carpet in your home or working premise and it is a wrong conception to think that only heavily soiled or badly stained carpet should require professional cleaning service.

Most common types of carpet stains.

General dirt and dust.

Prolonged use of carpet and rugs accumulates dirt, mud and dust posing as health hazards. It can be very damaging to your carpet as it blots out the beauty and cleanliness of your space and causes various allergies to you and people around you.

Water based stains.

Water based carpet stains are typically food, beverages such as beer, soft drinks, jelly, syrup, latex paint and many more. Combination oil and water-based stains such as salad dressing, chocolate, mayonnaise and many others also fall into this category.

Oil-based stains.

Oil-based carpet stains can be among the most difficult to remove because few consumer carpet stain removers are designed to remove such stains. Oil-based carpet and rug stains include adhesives, butter, cooking oils, crayon , glues, peanut butter and wax.

Juice and dye stains.

Carpet stains of this type are widely varied given that there are so many different types of dyes found in juices, foods and beverages and so forth. Time is essence here as these dyes can sometimes dye carpet fibres resulting in a permanent stain!

Pet stains and odour.

Even the most well-trained pet will have an occcasional accident or get sick and vomit on the carpet. Carpet stains in this category are organic materials such as urine, faeces and vomit.

How dirty carpets can affect you

Your carpet may make people in the office ill.

Your carpet acts as a filter, trapping damaging air pollutants like pollen, fungi, chemicals, bacteria, cigarette smoke tars and residues that you would otherwise be breathing in. These trapped pollutants can only be removed with regular special carpet cleaning techniques.

Your carpet may be bad news for asthma, allergy, eczema and Rhinitis sufferers.

Carpets, upholstery and mattresses can become infested with dust mites whose droppings can trigger asthma attacks and trap proteins known to trigger asthma, eczema and other allergy reactions.

Your dirty carpets may leave a bad impression for visitors to your office.

Whether its customers, suppliers or any visitors to your office, unsightly and dirty carpets will make your company look unprofessional.
