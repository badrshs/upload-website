About Segma Media

SegmaMedia is a large advertising network in the World, combining more than 120 website of leading publishers in the fields of business, sports, women, news, social media, automotive, and more. The company was founded in 2008 under the name GEO-Marketing, and was rebranded to SegmaMedia in 2010 after it has been fully acquired by Hinot Group.
