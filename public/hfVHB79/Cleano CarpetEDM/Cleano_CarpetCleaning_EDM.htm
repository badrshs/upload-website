Most common types of carpet stains

 

General Dirt and Dust

Prolonged use of carpet and rugs accumulates dirt, dust and mud
which can blot out the beauty and cleanliness of your space and
causes various allergies.

 

Oil Based Stains

Oil-based carpet stains such as oils and wax can be among the most difficult
to remove because Few consumer carpet stain removers are
designed to remove them.

 

Water Based Stains

Water based carpet stains are typically food and beverages such as beer,
soft drinks, jelly, syrup, chocolate and many more.

 

Juice and Dye Stains

There are a variety of dyes in fruits and beverages. Time is essence here
as dyes found in food colouring and juices can result in a permanent stain!

 

Pet Stains and Odour

Even the most well-trained pets will have an occcasional accident. Carpet
stains in this category include organic materials such as urine, faeces and vomit.
