Most common types of carpet stains .

General dirt and dust .

Prolonged use if carpet and rugs accumulates dirt , mud and dust posing as health hazards .It can be very damaging to your carpet as it blots out the beauty and cleanliness of your space and causes various allergies to you and people around you .

Water based stains .

Water based carpet stains are typically food , beverages such as beer , liquor and soft drinks ,baby formula , jelly , syrup , latex paint and many more .Combination oil and water-based stains such as salad dressing , oily foods of all types , chocolate , mayonnaise and many others also fall into this category .

Oil-based stains .

Oil-based carpet stains can be among the most difficult to remove because few consumer carpet stain removers are designed to remove such stains .Oil-based carpet and rug stains include adhesives , butter , cooking oils , crayon , glues , gravy ,grease , gum , peanut butter , wax and many others .

Juice and dye stains .

Carpet stains of this type are widely varied given that there are so many different types of dyes found in juices , foods and beverages and so forth. Time of this essence here as these dyes can sometimes dye carpet fibres resulting in a permanent stain !.

Pet stains and odour .

Even the most well-trained pet will an accasional accident or get sick and vomit on the carpet.Carpet stains in this category are organic materials such as urine , faeces , vomit and other organic materials .
