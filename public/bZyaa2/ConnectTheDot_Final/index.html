SEITAN

The Vegetarian Wheat Meat

Seitan is derived from the protein portion of wheat. It stands in for meat in many recipes with similar look and texture, and is also high in protein, making it a popular protein source for vegetarians.

TOFU

V
GF

Curd from mashed soybeans

Tofu is made by curdling fresh soya milk, pressing it into a solid block and then cooling it. It is an excellent source of amino acids, iron, calcium and other micro-nutrients, making it a versatile ingredient.
