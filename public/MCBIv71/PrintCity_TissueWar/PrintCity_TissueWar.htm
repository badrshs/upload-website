Did You Know? Tissue Advertising is the brand new
way of marketing.

Why Tissue Advertising?

1. The compact structure of tissue advertising is convenient as it allows many to carry it  everywhere we go. As such, tissue advertising is the optimal choice for your company as it attracts a large consumer base.

2.

Due to the increased frequency in usage, messages on tissue packs will be extremely prominent and memorable, serving corporations and small businesses well, especially due to its low start-up cost and high reachability to the targeted audience.

3.

With tissue advertising, it stimulates the visual senses of people through vibrant graphic printing, allowing us to boldly and clearly promote your purpose in this competitive industry today.

4.

By providing these tissues for free, it is more attractive to Singaporeans as they will not discard them. This ensures that they internalise the message on the packets and eventually increases their attention to your message or company.
