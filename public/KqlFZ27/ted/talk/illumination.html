Ahmed Amin

By telling relate-able short stories, comedian and actor Ahmed Amin reflects on how we over-calculate our life decisions, and how he was able to treat himself from this dilemma in an illuminating, talk about funny short stories from his life you've never heard before.

احمد امين ممثل وكاتب تخرج من كلية الفنون الجميلة عام 2002. عمل كممثل مسرحي لسنوات ثم اتجه للصحافة ليشغل منصب رئيس تحرير مجلة باسم للاطفال لنحو 10 سنوات، كتب للتلفزيون عدة مسلسلات من ابرزها: بسنت ودياسطي و القبطان عزوز. اشتهر امين مؤخرا بفيديوهات كوميدية على يوتيوب في سلسلة ال30 ثانية مثل "ازاي تطتب شعر عامية في 30 ثانية" فضلا عن بطولته وانتاجه لفيلم "حاضر مع المتهم"

Ahmed Amin is an actor and writer graduated from Faculty of Arts in 2002. Amin worked as a theatre actor, then the chief editor of Bassem children magazine. He wrote many TV serieses including "Basant w Dyasty" and "Captain Azuz". Amin is currently popular for his short youtube videos in the 30 seconds series like "How to write slang poetry in 30 seconds". He also starred and produced his short movie "Present with the Accused"

Eissa Fathi

A man is defined by what he's capable of doing, not by his certificates. Watch as Eissa Ali proves his abilities regardless of injustice, creating marvelous things using only a welding lamp and other simple equipment.

عيسى عامل مصري عمل بتصنيع ودهانات السيارات منذ الصغر. عمل في شركة جنرال موتورز في 2006 حتى 2009، تاركا العمل ليبدأ بمشروعه الخاص لتصميم السيارات تحت عنوان "عربيتك انتيكا"

Eissa Fathi is a car painter, and manufacturer. He learned this field in his childhood, in 2006 he worked in General Motors company till 2009. He now has his own car manufacturing company: "Arabeytak Antikka"

Enas Ahmed

Dr. Enas, an industrial engineer, with quality control degree, thought of how the Egyptian higher education system. She talked about the importance of analyzing the issue constructing an analyzing method to scientifically approach the problems called: 6 sigma, which can be applied to personal matters as well.

إيناس أحمد زكي حصلت على الماجستير في جودة التعليم في كلية الهندسة في عام 2014 إيناس تعمل حاليا كأخصائي جودة لتحسين نظام المناهج في جامعة الفيوم ليصبح نظام تقتدي به جامعات مصر. هي تعمل أيضا لتدريب المدرسين المساعدين وإعادة تأهيل الكليات لنشر الأبحاث العلمية باللغتين العربية والانجليزية.

Enas Ahmed earned her MA in Engineering Education Quality in 2014, she currently work as a Quality Specialist in applying and improving the quality of education in Fayoum University, hoping to create an integrated system to improve the Education quality that will set an example for other Egyptian Universities to follow, in addition to train teaching assistants and rehabilitate the colleges to earn a quality certificate and be able to create an online website to publish scientific researches, in both English and Arabic.

Adel Fakhry

We're all at battle with ourselves, at battle with what we think of ourselves, and what others think of us. Find out Adel's own battle with himself, how he ended it, and how it ultimately changed everything.

عادل كاتب موهوب وجد في الشعر خلاص من العالم الحقيقي. هو مؤمن بشدة بإمكانية إحداث تغيير حقيقي في العالم عن طريق كتاباته. هو مهتم بالفلسفة وعلم النفس والتصوير. تجاربه وقصصه تدور حول ذكريات حرب استغرقت سنين وانتهت بانتصاره على النفس.

Adel is a young talented writer who found peace and passion in writing poems as salvation from the real world. He believes that he can create actual change in the world through his writings. He's deeply interested in philosophy, psychology, and photography. His experiences are now stories of his memories of fights to conquer one-self.

Amal Salah

Have you ever suffered from comparison and for being different? Amal Salah, an artist, explores the magic of being different in a charming, heart to heart talk.

أمل صلاح فنانة اتخرجت من كلية الهندسة المعمارية مهتمة بتجربة المواد المختلفة وطريقة استخدامها. أمل بتعيد استعمال الحاجات العادية وبتبصلها من منظور مختلف لأنها مؤمنة بإن كل حاجة ليها قيمة بتختلف مع اختلاف المنظور. لوحاتها وأعمالها بتتضمن تجارب جديدة ومواد مختلفة زي الخشب الخيوط المسامير تذاكر النقل شرايط الكاسيت وحاجات تانية كتير.

Amal Salah is an architecture engineer. she's an artist that is interested in experimenting materials and installations. She believes that each material has a certain value depending on how you look at it. Her practice involves experimenting materials with different uses such as wood, threads, nails, transportation tickets, cassette tapes and so many other things.

Amir Tadros

Amir Tadros explores the new unique ways to approach social obligations and relationships. In this talk he particularly talks about how he cured his own son from Autism along with other surprisingly uncommon stories.

أمير تادروس يعمل حاليا مدير قطاع الموارد البشرية في الجامعة الامريكية, من هواياته الكتابة والموسيقى ويتبنى فكر جديد في تربية ابنائه.

Amir works as manager in the HR department in the American University in Cairo. His hobbies include writing and music. and he has developed a new method to approach to social relationships especially related to raising his own children.
