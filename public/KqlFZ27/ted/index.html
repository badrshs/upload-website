What is TED ?

TED is a nonprofit organization devoted to Ideas Worth Spreading. Started as a four-day conference in California 25 years ago, TED has grown to support those world-changing ideas with multiple initiatives. The annual TED Conference invites the world's leading thinkers and doers to speak for 18 minutes. Their talks are then made available, free, at TED.com. "TED" stands for Technology, Entertainment, Design - three broad subject areas that are, collectively, shaping our future. In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx - local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience.

What is TED ?

x = independently organized event In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized TED event. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events are self-organized.

What is TEDxWadiElRayan ?

TEDxWadiElRayan is an annually organized TEDx event, the first and only of its kind in Fayoum city. We have organized one event so far, under the theme: Illumination, hosting 200 attendees, and featuring 10 speakers like Ahmed Sayed Amin, Ahmed Nady, Eissa Fathy, Amal Salah, and others. Their videos and talks were uploaded on the TEDxTalks youtube channel making over 31,000 views. Our past event was held in April, 4th 2015, at the venue in the International Park. TEDxWadiElRayan hopes to discuss the ideas our community lacks through featuring an event in April 2016, to feature ideas like Mental Health misconceptions, encourage reading, Consumption and Recycling, Business Ethics, Technological discoveries and inventions, and many other topics and ideas.
