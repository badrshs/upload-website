Jobs Discription

The utmost priority of Imran Institute of Commerce & Science is to establish a discipline environment in the Institute and we can be able to achieve our goal with the honest contribution of our teaching staff towards the goal. Therefore, to achieve the goal, following are some disciplinary line of actions for teaching staff

Teacher’s Evaluation

Teachers are evaluated on the basis of:
►Class Room Behaviour
►Student's Result
►Peer's Feedback
►Student Feedback

Rights

►Students do not interfere with other students’ right to learn.
►Students’ should show courtesy, civility and respect in and out of the classroom, eg. Manners, speech, body language.t
►Students should show a willingness to participate in group work and activities.

Responsibilities

►To help students and parents in their dealings with school.
►To respect students and parents
►To be fair and consistent
►To encourage and reinforce good results and behaviors
►To apply consequences to misbehavior
►To be good role models
►To be conscientious in the duties
►To ensure the Code of Behavior is upheld
