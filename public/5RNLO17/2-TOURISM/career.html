    Orient Travel specialize in trips to the Orient and Africa . We are now looking for a travel agent to our office in Växjö.

    Have you worked as a travel agent a few years, result oriented, outgoing, enjoy working in teams and have their own experience of traveling to the Orient and Africa, you can be the one we seek.

    We expect you to have:

  • IATA training and a good knowledge of Sabre.
  • Good knowledge of the most common Windows programs.
  • Can work independently and are used to taking responsibility.
  • A genuine interest in our destinations.
  • Fluency in Swedish and English (both written and spoken).

    Applications should be sent to: ah@orientenresor.se
